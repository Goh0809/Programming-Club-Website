Welcome to our Flutter bootcamp! Embark on an exciting journey into cross-platform app development. Discover Flutter's power, master its UI toolkit, and build stunning mobile applications. This hands-on bootcamp equips you with the knowledge and tools to unleash your creativity for high-performance apps. To make the most of this immersive Flutter bootcamp, participants should come prepared with the following requirements :

  • 

    Participants should have a basic understanding of programming concepts, such as variables, functions, loops, and conditionals.

  • 

    Each participant is required to bring their own laptop or desktop computer with the necessary specifications to run Flutter smoothly.

  • 

    Flutter development is supported on Windows, macOS, and Linux. Participants should have one of these operating systems installed on their machines.

  • 

    It is recommended to have an IDE for Flutter development. Popular choices include Visual Studio Code, Android Studio, or IntelliJ IDEA with the Flutter and Dart plugins installed.

  • 

    Prior to the bootcamp, participants should have Flutter SDK and Dart programming language installed on their development environment. Instructions for installation will be provided before the bootcamp.

Welcome to the Flutter Bootcamp with Dart - created in collaboration with the Gogle Flutter Club.

Introducing Professor Xander Knight , a distinguished expert in the field of mobile app development and our esteemed guest lecturer at the Flutter bootcamp. With an impressive career spanning over two decades, Professor Knight has pioneered numerous groundbreaking projects and contributed to the advancement of cross-platform app development. Known for his dynamic teaching style and deep industry insights, he has inspired countless developers to push the boundaries of innovation. Get ready to learn from the best as Professor Knight shares his wealth of knowledge and expertise, guiding you towards app-building excellence

Who this bootcamp is for:

  • If you have never programmed before, then take this course to learn Dart and build iOS and Android apps.

  • If you know how to code, but are new to mobile development, then take this course to build apps with just one codebase.

  • If you are a native app developer, then take this course to see how much time you can save building native-quality iOS and Android apps with Flutter.

  • If you've tried Flutter before, then take this course to get a comprehensive curriculum that covers all the important aspects of Flutter development.
