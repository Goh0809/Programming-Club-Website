Experience the thrill of innovation and collaboration at the Hackathon Solution Challenge. Join us to solve real-world problems, showcase your skills, and transform ideas into impactful solutions alongside a dynamic community of passionate participants. To make the most of this immersive event, participants should come prepared with the following requirements :

  • 

    Participants should possess basic programming and software development skills relevant to the hackathon theme. Proficiency in languages such as Python, Java, JavaScript, or frameworks like React, Angular, or Django is beneficial.

  • 

    Participants are required to bring their own laptops or development devices with the necessary software and tools installed. Access to an internet connection and development environments is essential.

  • 

    Participants should approach the hackathon with an open mindset, ready to learn, collaborate, and adapt to new challenges. Embracing creativity, innovation, and a problem-solving attitude is highly encouraged.

  • 

    It is recommended to have an IDE for Hackathon Solution Challenge. Popular choices include Visual Studio Code, Visual Studio, or many others for this events

  • 

    Mandatory attendance at the designated Microsoft Teams Meeting for all participants. It provides crucial updates, announcements, and instructions. Failure to attend may lead to disqualification..

Welcome to the Hackathon Solution Challenge - created in collaboration with the Hackathon Club.

Introducing Scott N. Dahmen, the host of this event, is a passionate technologist and the proud owner of the Flutter Club. With extensive experience in software development and a deep understanding of Flutter, Scott is dedicated to promoting the Flutter framework and nurturing a vibrant community of developers. Known for his expertise in mobile app development, Scott is committed to sharing his knowledge, guiding aspiring developers, and fostering an environment of learning and collaboration. With his leadership and industry insights, Scott aims to inspire participants to unlock their full potential and excel in their Flutter journey.

Who this bootcamp is for:

  • High school and university students from various disciplines who are interested in gaining hands-on experience in coding, problem-solving, and team collaboration.

  • Individuals with a keen interest in technology, app development, and innovation, regardless of their professional background or experience level.

  • Professionals with specific domain knowledge, such as healthcare, finance, education, or sustainability, who can contribute valuable insights and help develop solutions tailored to specific industries or challenges.

  • Individuals who enjoy working in teams, building relationships, and leveraging diverse skills to collectively solve challenges and create impactful solutions.
