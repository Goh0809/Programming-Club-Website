Our Computing Programmes

In a world where technology has mostly taken over our everyday lives, we think it is crucial to be digitally literate. The expectation is that IT workers will foster innovation, significantly contribute to the economy, and positively influence society. With this in mind, our faculty of computing provides computer science related programmes. You will acquire the fundamental information and professional skills required to prepare you for work. Additionally, you'll discover how to identify and address issues before they arise.
